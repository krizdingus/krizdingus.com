About Krizdingus

I'm Kris, web developer by trade, maker by impulse. I build tools, toys, and quirks in equal measure, then share the blueprints here for anyone foolish enough to follow along.

I've been on the internet since 1995, back when I was slapping together shitty Angelfire and GeoCities pages to proclaim how awesome Mega Man and Gohan are. These days I still tinker, sometimes I record the chaos for Instagram, and call anything involving code, creative design, or crafting "a project."

After enough nudges from a handful of very kind people, I finally turned all this into a proper repository: project files, links, and more.

Thanks for stopping by!
